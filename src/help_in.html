  1. Tactical tic-tac-toe with moving pieces. Just click "Practice" and go for it. You should get the hang of it quickly.
  2. Two players alternate turns.
  3. The board is 5x5, with a 3x3 space for tic-tac-toe in the middle.
  4. You start with your pieces not on the board. Pieces can only be placed on the outside edge of the board. This takes one turn per piece.
  5. Pieces on the board can be moved one space at a time, in any direction. This also takes one turn per piece.
  6. If you move a piece, it pushes any pieces ahead of it out of the way. It is helpful to chain pieces in this manner.
  7. The name of the game is to get your opponent to move your pieces for you for free by their positioning.
  8. The edge of the board is like a wall.
  9. You have five pieces: 1 queen, 1 knight, and 3 pawns. They are large, medium, and small, respectively.
  10. Pieces can push other pieces of the same size or smaller. Which side the piece is on doesn't matter.
